Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is our best effort at making a delicious and affordable homemade lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the moodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
